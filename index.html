Who are we.

Align Technologies Pte Ltd was founded in September 1999 with a clear vision: to become a trusted IT solutions provider in Singapore. From its inception, Align Technologies focused on delivering value-added services, ensuring that clients received not just products, but tailored solutions that met their unique business needs.

The company has established strong partnerships with global IT brands such as Lenovo, Dell, HPE, Microsoft, Trend Micro, Dropbox, Fortinet, and Cisco, offering high-quality hardware and software solutions to small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across various industries.
